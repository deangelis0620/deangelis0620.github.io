First Movie

Just a few years after the emergence of filmmakers in the mid-1890s, George Mellies created "La Manoir du Diable", better known in English as "The Haunted Castle" or "The House of the Devil", in 1896. It is considered to be the first horro movie. In the three-minute film, there are cauldrons, animated skeletons, transforming bats, and the incarnation of the Devil.
